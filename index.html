About Us

Swift Tech LLC is an authorized distributor of electronics and aerospace parts and hardware that specializes in providing quality brand-name products. Our sales team has over Thirty-Five years combined experience in distribution. We are committed to providing the best service to our customers. Our staff understands not only your needs, but the technical aspects of the products as well. Our goal is to maintain quality assurance, on time delivery, competitive pricing with integrity and total reliability for both domestic and international customers. Our 20,000 square foot facility includes a climate controlled inventory room that currently holds over 5 million in-stock items. We also are registered with the Dept. of State which enables us to secure export licenses in a fast, timely manner.
